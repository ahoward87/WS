Small Groups for Adults

What We Offer

Agape

The Agape senior women’s adult Bible study meets every Sunday morning at 8:15 AM in Room 136. Taught by Pat Soderholm this group will begin a new study entitled, "Battlefield of the Mind" in September. Worry, doubt, confusion, depression, anger and feelings of condemnation are attacks on the mind. Learn how to change your life by changing your mind and focusing on the victory you have in Christ.

Young Married Bible Class (YMBC)

YMBC (Couples in their 20's & 30's) will start a new 11-week lesson series entitled "Real Marriage" by Mark & Grace Driscoll. Topics include "New Marriage, Same Spouse", "Friend with Benefits", "Taking Out the Trash", "Disgrace & Grace". Join us on Sunday mornings at 9:30 AM in Room 138.

CrossRoads

Designed for single adults 20’s & 30’s, the CrossRoads young adult Bible study meets Sunday mornings at 9:30 AM in Room 232 (2nd Floor Conference Room). Taught by Paul Haines, the class studies the Bible as it relates to the unique challenges of singles. For more information, email crossroads@communitycc.com.

Faith in Action

Designed for all adults age 35+, Faith in Action (FIA) meets on Sunday mornings at 11:00 AM in Room 138. Led by John Chace, FIA will begin a study of the Book of Mark on September 15. Experience Mark’s vivid account of Jesus’ compassion-filled ministry as we focus on Jesus’ teaching, miracles, parables, and ultimately on His sacrificial death and resurrection. For more information, email jchace@communitycc.com.
