Striving To Provide

Oasis Resources

Need a book on parenting? Maybe a new Bible? Or perhaps a gift for someone special?

Oasis Resources, located in the Atrium, next to our Cafe, offers a broad selection of Bibles, CDs, DVDs, seasonal gift items and books, including devotional and fiction.

Additionally, you can purchase a recording of Sunday's message on CD immediately following each of our two worship services.

Adult Small Groups

At Community Christian Church, we believe that people thrive in small groups. That is why we offer a number of different Bible study related settings to adults and young adults.

Community Kidz

We think Kidz learn better when they're having fun. That is why we have formed our Community Kidz program! If kids have fun while learning, they're more likely to stick with it. We look forward to meeting your Kidz!
